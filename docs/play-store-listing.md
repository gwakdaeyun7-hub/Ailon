## Section 1: App Title

**Recommended title (30 chars max):**

```
AILON - AI News & Insights
```

(26 characters)

**Rationale:** "AILON" alone is not descriptive enough for discoverability. Adding "AI News & Insights" immediately communicates the value proposition and includes two high-value ASO keywords. Alternative considered: "AILON - AI News Digest" (22 chars) — slightly narrower, omits the learning dimension.

## Section 3: Full Description

```
Stay ahead with AILON (AI Insight Linked On Network) — the smartest way to follow artificial intelligence news.

Every day, AILON collects articles from 22 trusted AI and tech sources including Wired, TechCrunch, MIT Technology Review, VentureBeat, The Verge, and more. Our AI pipeline reads, translates, and summarizes each article so you get the essential information in minutes, not hours.

WHAT YOU GET EVERY DAY

-- AI-Curated News Feed --
Articles from 22 sources, organized into three clear categories: Research, Models & Products, and Industry & Business. Each article includes a headline summary, three key points, context on why it matters, background information, and a glossary of technical terms. No fluff, no clickbait — just the facts that matter.

-- Daily Briefing --
A 2-3 minute AI-generated briefing that captures the day's most important AI stories. Read it with your morning coffee or listen with built-in text-to-speech. Includes trend highlights and category breakdowns so you always know where the industry is heading.

-- Daily Learning: Principles Behind AI --
Go beyond the headlines. Every day, AILON presents one academic principle from fields like mathematics, physics, information theory, or optimization — and shows you exactly how it connects to real AI technology. Each lesson follows a three-step format: Foundation (the core concept), Application (the AI connection), and Integration (real-world impact). Includes a Deep Dive section for those who want to go further.

-- Smart Glossary --
Technical terms are automatically highlighted throughout the app. Tap any term to see a clear, jargon-free definition. Your glossary grows as you read, building your AI vocabulary over time.

KEY FEATURES

- 22 curated sources from leading AI and technology publications
- AI-generated summaries with key points, context, and background
- Daily briefing with text-to-speech playback
- Daily academic insights spanning 12 scientific disciplines
- Bilingual support: English and Korean
- Highlights section featuring the top story from each category
- Related articles and timeline showing past coverage of the same topic
- Bookmarks to save articles and learning content for later
- Comments and reactions to discuss articles with the community
- Dark mode and light mode
- Push notifications for breaking AI news
- Completely free with no ads

OUR SOURCES

AILON draws from a carefully selected mix of international publications:

Tier 1: Wired AI, TechCrunch AI, The Verge, MIT Technology Review, VentureBeat, MarkTechPost, The Decoder, AI Business, SiliconANGLE, The Next Web, TechXplore, Tom's Hardware

Tier 2: Google DeepMind Blog, NVIDIA Blog, Hugging Face Blog, Ars Technica AI, The Rundown AI, IEEE Spectrum AI

Korean Sources: AI Times, GeekNews, ZDNet Korea AI, Yozm IT

HOW IT WORKS

Our automated pipeline runs twice daily. It collects articles via RSS and web scraping, filters for AI relevance, translates non-English content, generates structured summaries using large language models, categorizes and ranks articles by importance, extracts key entities and topics, and identifies related coverage — all before delivering a clean, organized feed to your phone.

WHO IS AILON FOR

- AI practitioners and researchers who need a daily digest
- Tech professionals tracking industry developments
- Students and lifelong learners exploring the science behind AI
- Anyone curious about artificial intelligence and machine learning

Download AILON and never miss what matters in AI.
```
