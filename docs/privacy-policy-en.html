1. Introduction

AILON — AI Insight Linked On Network — (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is an AI news curation and interdisciplinary learning service operated by David Jung (정다윗). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects only the minimum personal information necessary to provide the service.

Data Collected Collection Method Purpose
Email address Google Sign-In Account identification and authentication
Display name Google Sign-In Display within the service (e.g., comments)
Profile photo URL Google Sign-In Profile display within the service
Device push token Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) and Expo Push Notifications Delivering push notifications
Usage activity data In-app activity Providing service features: likes, bookmarks, article views, comments

The App does NOT collect: location data, contacts, photos or videos, microphone or camera access, health data, financial information, or any other sensitive personal information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Account management: Registration, login authentication, and account identification
  • Service delivery: AI news curation, interdisciplinary learning content, personalized feed
  • Feature operation: Likes, bookmarks, comments, sharing, and other interactive features
  • Notifications: Push notifications for new articles, comment replies, and likes
  • Service improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve content quality and user experience

The App does not contain advertisements. We do not use your personal information for advertising purposes.

4. AI-Generated Content

Article summaries, daily briefings, and learning content displayed in this App are generated by artificial intelligence (Google Gemini). We do not store or reproduce original article text. All AI-generated summaries link back to the original source articles published by their respective authors and publishers.

AI-generated content may contain inaccuracies. Users should refer to original sources for authoritative information before making decisions based on content presented in the App.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

However, your information may be shared in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use Google Firebase for data storage, authentication, and cloud functions. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Publicly shared information: Information you voluntarily make public, such as comments and display names, will be visible to other users.

6. Data Retention

Personal information is retained for the following periods:

Data Type Retention Period
Account information (email, name, profile) Until account deletion is requested
Usage activity data (likes, bookmarks, comments) Until account deletion is requested
Device push tokens Until notifications are disabled or account is deleted

While we implement commercially reasonable security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
